Great question! In projectile motion, gravity acts only vertically — it has zero horizontal component. So the horizontal velocity vₓ = v₀cosθ stays constant throughout the flight. AI Mentor · just now
Meanwhile, the vertical velocity changes as vᵧ = v₀sinθ − gt. Because no force acts horizontally, Newton's first law keeps vₓ unchanged. These two motions are completely independent — that's the superposition principle! 🚀 AI Mentor · just now
